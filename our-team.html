Dr. Mayank Singh

Founder & CEO, IMPUTE Inc.,

Dr. Mayank Singh was educated in India and Japan and graduated with a doctorate in Electrical, Electronics and Communication Engineering from Gunma University, Japan. He has also completed a Master’s in Business Administration (MBA) from Temple University, Fox School of Business Management. He has worked with IBM and Sumitomo Bakelite in the area of high-performance computing. In 2017, Mayank decided to pursue his passion in AI and Digital therapeutics by founding IMPUTE Inc., a Tokyo based company, in 2017.

Hirofumi Hamanaka

Co-Founder

Hirofumi Hamanaka has over 30 years of experience in the field of healthcare and pharmaceuticals. His main expertise is in the field of clinical trials. Over the years he has worked for and lead product divisions for various pharma companies, including Pfizer Ikon. He is based in Tokyo and has a master’s degree in pharmacy.
